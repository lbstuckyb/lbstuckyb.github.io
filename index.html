Services

We remain dedicated to advancing the understanding of the use of kinetics in elite sport through ongoing applied research.

Force Plate Education

Structured or bespoke education on the use of force platform technology in the profiling and monitoring of the healthy athlete and of injured players during rehabilitation, return-to-sport and beyond. Education includes on-site or virtual CPD sessions/workshops or a structured program designed for performance and / or medical staff and may be linked to formal qualifications.

Practitioner Mentorship

Mentorship of individuals or groups of practitioners - tailored to the practical and educational needs and constraints of the team. This service will enhance and support the implementation of existing or new profiling/monitoring processes. Practitioners/teams undertaking internal or academic research projects can also be supported – via formal supervision or advice.

Data Analysis and Interpretation

A full force platform data analysis, visualization and reporting service for team profiling and or monitoring. Raw data – collected using any force platform system - can be processed and visualized in minutes and anomalies alerts highlighted. Results are provided as a report with or without follow-up call with performance/medical staff to discuss analyzed trends/profiles.
